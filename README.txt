ON LAUNCH:
1. LEMON TREE IS DISPLAYED.
2. TAP LEMON TREE TO SELECT LEMON.

ON TAPPING TREE:
1. LEMON IS DISPLAYED.
2. TAP LEMON TO SQUEEZE.
3. LONG PRESS LEMON TO SHOW SQUEEZE COUNT.

ON SQUEEZING LEMON:
1. GLASS IS FILLED WITH LEMONADE.
2. TAP GLASS TO DRINK.

ON DRINKING:
1. GLASS BECOMES EMPTY.
2. TAP EMPTY GLASS TO RESTART.
